BIO

My name is Andrei, I'm a 16 y/o from Romania. I grew up in a pretty boring town full of old people so I didn't make much friends. I grew around people who made fun of you because what you liked isn't deemed "popular" which made me kind of close myself and never reveal any of my passions. For most of my childhood I didn't play much videogames since my parents were much stricter when I was little. I used to be kind of an introvert and didn't talk much but the quarantine and the hell that is online school (aka staying in a room for 8 hours straight in front of your computer) made me admire the nicer side of life and talk to people more. I like walks in the park and admiring the smallest of details in everything around me.




The pandemic made me realise how retarded social media in today's age is and I think the idea of Web 1.0 and personal sites is much MUCH better. People express themselves much more freely and you can really see their personalities shine through elements of the site.

Despite my evergrowing hatred toward social media (especially instagram and twitter), I do have a discord account, if ye wanna chat, feel free to dm me at: Head Priest#3776.
